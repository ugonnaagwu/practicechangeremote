Roses

If you're looking to add a touch of enchantment to your garden, the Blue Moon Rose is an excellent option. Though true blue roses are rare in nature, the Blue Moon Rose comes close with its breathtaking lavender-blue petals. This hybrid tea rose is not only visually stunning but also emits a delightful fragrance. Its long stems make it an ideal choice for cut flower arrangements. Black Baccara Rose

Roses

For those with a taste for the dramatic, the Black Baccara Rose is a perfect choice. This velvety dark red rose exudes an air of mystery and elegance. Its deep, almost black petals make a striking contrast against the green foliage, creating a captivating visual display. The Black Baccara Rose is known for its strong fragrance, which adds an extra dimension to its allure. This rose variety thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and vigor.
